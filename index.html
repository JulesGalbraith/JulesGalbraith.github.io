Jules Galbraith is a Montreal based artist and writer working across poetics, creative web development, audio-visual media, and editorial design. They are interested in para-academic, peer-to-peer networks of research & knowledge transmisson. Jules is a founding member of the Cyber Love Hotel, a community-oriented tech studio, and the founder of Amor Mundi (TBA 2023) an publishing initiative focused on poetry, translation, and polemics. Their artistic interests are love, grief, and (non)being.
