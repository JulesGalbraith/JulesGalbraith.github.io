Jules Galbraith is a Montreal based artist and writer working across poetics, creative web development, audio-visual media, and editorial design. They are interested in para-academic and horizontal social networks of research, knowledge sharing, and production. They are a longtime member of the Cyber Love Hotel, a community-oriented tech studio, and the founder of Amor Mundi, to be launched in 2023, an independent press focused on poetry, translation, and other forms of idiosyncratic writing.
