Jules Galbraith is a Montreal based artist and writer working across poetics, creative web development, A/V, and editorial design. Jules is an original member of cyberlove, a community-oriented tech studio, and runs Amor Mundi, an independent publishing initiative focused on poetry, translation, and polemics. Their artistic interests are love, grief, and (non)being. They are interested in forming para-academic, peer-to-peer networks of knowledge-making and sharing.
