Jules Galbraith is a Montreal based artist and writer working across poetics, risograph printing, creative web development, 3D, and editorial design. They are a founding member of cyberlove, a community-oriented tech studio, where they facilitate the formation of para-academic, peer-to-peer networks of knowledge exchange. They run Amor Mundi, an independent publishing initiative focused on poetry, translation, and polemics.
